Description:

The recipes included in this meal focus on priotizing a high protein intake and low calorie to align with my fitness goals.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 breasts Chicken breast raw
  2. 135g Lettuce
  3. 35g Ketchup
  4. Oikos 2% milkfat Greek yoghurt
  5. 4 Wheat tortilla(you can use any type if you do not like wheat)

Steps:

  • Spice chicken with your favorite spices and let it sit for some time.
  • Mix yoghurt and ketchup then add spices of choice.
  • Cook chicken on oiled pan
  • Slice lettuce
  • spread yoghurt sauce on tortilla
  • Add lettuce and chicken to the tortilla.
