Description:

The recipes included in this meal focus on priotizing high protein intake while being low in calories that will align with your

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cubes Ice
  2. 90g Frozen Banana
  3. 6tbsp Peanut Butter Powder
  4. 2tbsp Oats
  5. 2 scoops Protein powder
  6. 4tbsp Cocoa Powder
  7. 1/2tsp Vanilla extract
  8. 1 sachet Sweetener
  9. Water(Add based on how thick you want the smoothie to be)

Steps:

  • Add dry ingredients in a bowl and mix with water
  • Pour mixture in blender
  • Add frozen banan and ice
  • Enjoy
