Description:

The recipes included in this meal focus on priotizing a high protein intake and low calorie to align with my fitness goals.

Ingredients:

  1. Beef ground 90% lean 10%fat raw
  2. 250g Mixed vegetables
  3. 300 ml Diced tomatoes by Aylmer
  4. 340g Penne rigate by Lancia
  5. 1.2 cups 2% fat cottage cheese by Kirland Signature

Steps:

  • Boil Pasta
  • Blend the Cottage Cheese and tomateoes alongside your favorite spices.
  • Stir fry the Lean ground beef with mixed vegetables and your favorite spices.
  • Dry out the water from the boiled pasta
  • Mix the pasta,cottage cheese blend and the lean ground beef together.
